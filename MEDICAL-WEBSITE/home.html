ABOUT US
The Selection Committee, Directorate of Medical Education and Research is authority to make admissions for All Medical, Dental & Paramedical courses in Government Colleges and Government Quota seats in Self Financing Colleges. It functions under the control of Director of Medical Education and Research as a chairperson and the Secretary in the cadre of Additional Director of Medical Education to look after its activities exclusively.
